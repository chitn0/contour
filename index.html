October 2015 Publication students have come together to create a publication that pays homage to the world around them, and this website is the home of those works. This issue of Contour aims to reflect aspects of the world as experienced by our students. By sharing our experiences, creativity, and perspectives, we at Contour hope that you, our reader, are able to better see the world that we share. Contour is about people and culture and earth, and it is also about you.
