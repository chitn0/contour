nutella kiffles

Ingredients

  • 1/4 kg ricotta
  • 1/4 kg margarine
  • 500g Self-raising flour sifted
  • Pinch of salt
  • Jar of Nutella

Method

  • To begin, pre-heat oven to 180 degrees.
  • Cream the ricotta, margarine and salt in a mix master.
  • Add flour slowly and then once the pastry is too thick for the beater start kneading it with your hands on a floured board.
  • Continue kneading the pastry until it becomes a smooth consistency. Split the pastry into 8-10 parts.
  • Roll out the ball until it becomes a thin flat circle. Be sure not to roll it too thinly as it needs to hold the Nutella.
  • With a butter knife cut the circle of pastry into eighths like you would if you were cutting a pizza.
  • Grab your teaspoon and place half a spoonful of Nutella at the outer edge of each of the 'pizza' shaped pastry slices.
  • Roll from the outside edge into the middle and then curl the edges in.
  • Place on baking trays and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
    • Recipe makes between 50-60 little Nutella kiffels.
    • *Helpful hint* To make it healthier just replace the ricotta and margarine with a low fat option of themselves! It will taste just as good!!

banana bread

Ingredients

  • Melted butter, to grease
  • 125g butter, chopped, softened
  • 155g (3/4 cup, firmly packed) brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, at room temperature
  • 3 large overripe bananas, mashed
  • 200g (1 1/2 cups) self-raising flour, sifted
  • 160ml (2/3 cup) milk

Method

  • Brush a 1.75L (7-cup) capacity microwave-safe plastic ring cake pan with melted butter to grease.
  • Use an electric beater to beat together the butter and sugar in a large bowl until pale and creamy.
  • Add the eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each addition until combined. Stir in the banana.
  • Stir in half the flour and milk until just combined. Repeat with remaining flour and milk.
  • Spoon into prepared pan and smooth the surface.
  • Place on a microwave-safe rack or upturned plate and cook, uncovered, on Medium/500watts/50% for 14 minutes or until a skewer inserted into centre of cake comes out clean (it will be a little sticky on top).
  • Set aside in the pan for 3 minutes. Turn onto a plate and set aside for 30 minutes to cool.

banana berry smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries
  • 3 tbsp. natural yoghurt
  • 1tbsp. chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp. smoothie powder

Method

  • Cut banana into slices.
  • Place banana into a mixing jug, add milk, yoghurt, berries, chia seeds and powder.
  • Blend together using an electric mixer or smoothie blender until smooth.
  • Pour into a glass or mug.

milo bowl cake

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbls Milo
  • 4 tbls sugar
  • 4 tbls flour
  • 4 tbls milk
  • 4 tbls oil
  • 1 egg

Method

  • Gather a small size bowl and grease it up with butter or cooking spray
  • Place the milo, sugar, flour, milk, oil and egg into the bowl.
  • Stir ingredients until all combined.
  • Place in a microwave for 3 minutes.
  • Take about and serve with a dollop of ice cream.

spaghetti bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g Woolworths select spaghetti
  • 3 garlic gloves, crushed
  • 500g mince beef
  • 4 tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons of salt & pepper
  • 575g Leggos pasta
  • Bolognese sauce
  • 1 tomato

Method

  • Boil water with two tbsp. of olive oil and two pinches of salt in a medium pot over medium heat until boiling. Add 400g Woolworths select spaghetti and cook for 20 minutes. Drain.
  • Add 2 tbsp. olive oil to medium pot and cook garlic for 3 to 4 minutes or until garlic has slightly browned. Add tomatoes and cook for 3 minutes or until softened.
  • Increase heat to medium-high. Roll up mince into balls and add mince. Cook, stirring with a wooden spoon and careful not to break up mince, for 8 to 10 minutes or until browned. Add the pasta sauce and stir until boiling.
  • Add in cooked spaghetti, mix in with the pasta sauce and beef mince until spaghetti thoroughly covered.
  • Serve with Camembert cheese or mozzarella cheese and pepper.

bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 3 regular size tomatoes diced
  • 1 Spanish onion, diced
  • 1 white Pane di Casa loaf of bread (thick)
  • 10g of fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 20mL olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove finely chopped
  • 1 small jar of pesto
  • Shaved parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Method

  • Preheat oven grill to 200*C.
  • Add tomatoes, Spanish onion, basil, olive oil, garlic and a pinch of salt and pepper in a large mixing bowl. Stir until combined.
  • Spread a thin layer of pesto across four slices of bread and place in the oven for approximately 2 minutes or until crispy.
  • Place bruschetta mix on top of bread and garnish with shaved parmesan cheese.
